640x 480p (60Hz refresh rate)

Total number of pixels:
Horizontal - 800 pixels (640 of those pixels are for addressable video time, signal go high when displaying colors)
Vertical - 524 pixels   (480 of those pixels "									 ")


Another important aspect when pixel count starts
2 pixels in the beginning indicating vertical sync time (for that amount of time the signal for VSYNC will go high)
96 pixels "					       "(for that amount of time the signal for HSYNC will go high)
